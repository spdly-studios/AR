About AkshaRaksha

Building Resilient Communication Networks

Our Vision

AkshaRaksha is a resilient satellite-assisted emergency communication network designed to ensure that no person becomes unreachable during a disaster. We combine cutting-edge satellite technology with intelligent mesh networking to create communication systems that work when traditional infrastructure fails.

What We Do

We develop end-to-end communication systems that bridge the gap between emergency response and affected populations. Our approach combines:

  • Satellite-assisted communication networks
  • Resilient mesh networking protocols
  • Real-time signal processing
  • Custom ground station technology
  • Humanitarian-focused infrastructure design

Why It Matters

During disasters, communication infrastructure is often the first casualty. People lose the ability to call for help, access critical information, or reconnect with loved ones. AkshaRaksha changes this by creating communication systems that are inherently resilient and don't depend on centralized infrastructure.

Our Approach

We apply rigorous engineering principles to humanitarian challenges. Every component is designed with a focus on:

  • Reliability: Systems that work when they're needed most
  • Latency: Real-time communication for urgent situations
  • Accessibility: Technology that reaches those in need
  • Sustainability: Solutions that can be deployed and maintained long-term
