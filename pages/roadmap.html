Development Roadmap

Building the Future of Emergency Communication

Phase 1: Research & Prototyping (2025-2026)

  • Foundational research on satellite communication protocols
  • Development of adaptive DSP algorithms for signal processing
  • Prototype ground station design
  • Mesh networking protocol development
  • Signal integrity and reliability analysis

Status: In Progress

Phase 2: Hardware Development (2026-2027)

  • PCB design and manufacturing of communication units
  • Integration of satellite communication modules
  • Custom ground station hardware development
  • Power electronics optimization for field deployment
  • Comprehensive testing and validation

Status: Planning

Phase 3: System Integration (2027-2028)

  • End-to-end system testing and validation
  • Deployment in controlled environments
  • Real-world scenario simulations
  • Standardization of protocols and procedures
  • Documentation and knowledge transfer

Status: Planning

Phase 4: Field Deployment (2028+)

  • Pilot deployments in disaster-prone regions
  • Collaboration with humanitarian organizations
  • Real-world disaster response support
  • Continuous improvement based on field data
  • Scaling and global expansion

Status: Future

Key Technologies

  • LEO and MEO satellite communication links
  • Adaptive DSP algorithms
  • RTOS-based embedded systems
  • Real-time mesh networking
  • Software-defined radio (SDR) technology
  • Custom telemetry and data pipelines
